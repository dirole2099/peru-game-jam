Create games. Discover Peru.

A seven-day international creative retreat combining travel, local culture and collaborative game development in Peru.

Four cities. Four experiences.
One journey.

Lima Edition

A coastal, urban edition built around food, contemporary culture and Peru's largest game development community. The ideal entry point into the journey.

Cusco Edition

A high-altitude creative retreat shaped by Andean history, living traditions and one of the world's most extraordinary cultural landscapes.

Arequipa Edition

Surrounded by volcanoes and built from white volcanic stone, Arequipa offers space to think, create and collaborate at a different pace.

Iquitos Edition

Deep in the Amazon, where rivers replace roads and nature reshapes perspective. An edition designed to spark curiosity, experimentation and unforgettable stories.
